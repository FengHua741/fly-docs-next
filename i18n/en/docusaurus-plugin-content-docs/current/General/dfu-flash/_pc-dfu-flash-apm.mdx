1. Flashing Tool <Button variant="contained" disableElevation href="https://mellow-next.klipper.cn/docs/ResDownload/auxiliary_software/">Download Link</Button>
2. Download `DFUProgrammer`
3. Copy the firmware (klipper.bin) compiled in advance or the pre-compiled `Katapult firmware` to any directory on your computer
4. Connect the main board to the computer using a Type-C cable, and make sure it has entered **DFU flashing mode**
5. Open DFUProgrammer and follow the instructions below
    <ImageView image={require('@site/docs/General/dfu-flash/img/apmdfu.webp').default} size="100%" align="center" />
    <ImageView image={require('@site/docs/General/dfu-flash/img/apmdfu1.webp').default} size="100%" align="center" />
6. Click to start updating the firmware and wait for it to complete, then you can remove the Type-C cable and jumpers.
