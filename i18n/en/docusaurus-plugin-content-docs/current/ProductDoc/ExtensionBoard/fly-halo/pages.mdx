## Extrude Page

    <ImageView image={require('@site/docs/ProductDoc/ExtensionBoard/fly-halo/img/jichu-1.webp').default} size="50%" align="left" />
    <ImageView image={require('@site/docs/ProductDoc/ExtensionBoard/fly-halo/img/jichu-2.webp').default} size="50%" align="left" />
    * The operable areas in the image above correspond to the numbers below
    1. Click: Increase the extruder head target temperature by 1
    2. Long press: Enter the extrusion temperature setting, adjust the temperature by dragging the ring bar, and click again to confirm the set temperature
    3. Click: Decrease the extruder head target temperature by 1
    4. Click: Extrude
    5. Swipe up/down: Select the length of extrusion or retraction, unit mm
    6. Click: Retract
    7. Long press: Enter the extrusion speed setting in the image above, swipe up/down to select the extrusion speed, unit mm/s, and click the surrounding blank area to exit
