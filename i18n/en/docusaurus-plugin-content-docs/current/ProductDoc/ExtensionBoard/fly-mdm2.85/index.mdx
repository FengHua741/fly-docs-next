# Product Introduction

  - MDM filament sensor, small size, easy to use, and highly sensitive trigger.
  - Applicable for 3D printers such as Klipper, Marlin, RRF, etc.
  - Filament out detection: High-speed infrared scanning, captures the moment of filament breakage within 0.25 seconds, immediately pauses printing and sounds an alarm, avoiding wasted prints;
  - Blockage warning: Encoder optical signal recognition, identifies precursors of nozzle/hotend blockage, reducing scrap rate by over 80%.
  - Wide compatibility: Supports all types of filaments (2.85mm diameter) including PLA, ABS, PETG, TPU, etc., whether rigid or flexible materials, can be stably identified;
  - Easy installation: Provides mounting hole diagrams, wiring diagrams, and configuration files to facilitate installation and usage.
