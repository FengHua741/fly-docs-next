## Entering DFU Flash Mode

    :::tip

    The left is the `BOOT button`, and the right is the `RESET button`.

    :::

    * There are two methods to enter flash mode
    * First method: With the motherboard completely powered off, press and hold the BOOT button and then connect the data transfer cable to the motherboard and your device
    * Second method: After connecting the data transfer cable to the motherboard and your device, press and hold the BOOT button and then press the RST button, release the RST button, and finally release the BOOT button
