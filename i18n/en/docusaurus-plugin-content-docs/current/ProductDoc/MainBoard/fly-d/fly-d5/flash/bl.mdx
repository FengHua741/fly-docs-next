## Enter DFU Flash Mode

    :::tip

    Left is the `BOOT button`, right is the `RESET button`

    :::

    * There are two methods to enter flash mode
    * First method: With the motherboard completely powered off, press and hold the BOOT button, then connect the data transfer cable to the motherboard and your device
    * Second method: After connecting the data transfer cable to the motherboard and your device, press and hold the BOOT button and then press the RST button, release the RST button first, then release the BOOT button
