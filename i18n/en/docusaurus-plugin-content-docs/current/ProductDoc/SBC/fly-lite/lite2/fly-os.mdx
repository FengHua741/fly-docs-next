## Preparation Before System Burn-in

    * Card Reader
    * One TF card with a capacity of no less than 16GB or no more than 128GB, the transfer speed of the TF card must be Class10 or above
    * Download the corresponding <Button variant="contained" disableElevation href="/docs/ResDownload/system-img/fly-lite2">System Image</Button>
    * Download the required <Button variant="contained" disableElevation href="/docs/ResDownload/auxiliary_software/">Burning Tool</Button>

## System Burn-in

:::info
* Lite 2 can only burn the system to the TF card and cannot use M2WE
* After the system is burned in, the TF card needs to be installed on Lite 2
