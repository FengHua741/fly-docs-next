## Preparation Before System Burn-in

    * Card Reader
    * One TF card with capacity not less than 16GB or not greater than 128GB, and the transfer speed of the TF card must be Class10 or above
    * Download the corresponding <Button variant="contained" disableElevation href="/docs/ResDownload/system-img/fly-minipad">System Image</Button>
    * Download the required <Button variant="contained" disableElevation href="/docs/ResDownload/auxiliary_software/">Burn-in Tool</Button>

## System Burn-in

:::info
* MINI PAD can only burn the system to the TF card and cannot use M2WE.
* After the system burn-in is completed, the TF card needs to be installed on the MINI PAD.
:::
